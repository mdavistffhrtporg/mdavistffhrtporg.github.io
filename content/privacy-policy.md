# Privacy Policy

## Overview

This website (`matthewd.org`) is a personal website operated by Matthew Davis.

The site is designed to be simple, secure, and respectful of visitor privacy. It does not sell personal data, does not run advertising networks, and does not engage in behavioral profiling or targeted advertising.

This policy explains what information may be processed when you visit or interact with the site, how it is used, and which third parties may be involved as a result of your participation.

---

## Information Processed Automatically

When you access this website, limited technical information may be processed automatically to ensure secure and reliable operation. This may include:

- IP address
- HTTP request headers
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Requested URLs
- Date and time of requests
- Referring URL

This information is processed **only for security, abuse prevention, performance, and operational integrity**, consistent with common content delivery and infrastructure protection practices.

This website does **not** use advertising trackers, cross-site profiling, or marketing analytics.

---

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website does not set first-party cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

Third-party services used on the site may set cookies or similar technologies when you interact with their features. These technologies are governed by the privacy policies of the respective third parties.

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data.

### GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the right to:
- Access personal data concerning you
- Request correction or deletion of personal data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Processing of limited technical data is based on **legitimate interests** in operating, securing, and maintaining the website.

---

### CCPA / CPRA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Request deletion of personal information
- Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information

This website does **not** sell or share personal information for commercial purposes.

---

### Texas Data Privacy and Security Act (TDPSA)

If you are a Texas resident, you have the right to:
- Confirm whether personal data is being processed
- Access or delete personal data
- Opt out of certain processing activities

This website processes only limited technical data necessary for secure operation and does not engage in targeted advertising or profiling.
