## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability or have a security-related concern regarding this website or its associated repositories, please report it responsibly.

### Preferred Contact

- **Email:** matthewd@matthewd.org

You may encrypt your message using the public PGP key available at:

- **PGP Key:** https://matthewd.org/mdavis.asc

### Alternate Contact

If email is unavailable, you may also contact:

- **Email:** mdavisa2021@gmail.com

---

## Scope

This security policy applies to:

- https://matthewd.org
- Associated GitHub repositories related to the website
- Site configuration, build pipelines, and deployment workflows

It does **not** apply to third-party services or platforms used by the site, which are governed by their own security policies.

---

## What to Include in a Report

To help resolve issues efficiently, please include:

- A clear description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or proof of concept
- The affected URL(s) or component(s)

---

## Expectations

- Please allow reasonable time for investigation and response.
- Do not publicly disclose the issue before it has been reviewed and addressed.
- This site does not operate a bug bounty program, but responsible disclosures are appreciated and will be acknowledged when appropriate.

---

## Safe Harbor

Security research and responsible disclosure conducted in good faith, without exploiting data or disrupting services, are welcomed and will not result in legal action.

---

Thank you for helping keep this site secure.
